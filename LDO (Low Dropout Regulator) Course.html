LDO (Low Dropout Regulator) Course

What You'll Learn

This course focuses on the principles and practical aspects of designing LDO circuits. It combines theoretical concepts with hands-on experience, drawn from years of engineering expertise. The course is structured into four major sections: Requirements, Design, Test, and Layout Recommendations.

You will learn:

  • Understand and analyze the specifications for your LDO.
  • Select the ideal LDO for your application needs.
  • Choose the right components such the input and output capacitors.
  • Choose capacitors with low ESR for better noise rejection and stability.
  • Analyse the DC Bias because it can affect the capacitance, especially in ceramic capacitors.
  • Calculate and analyze the Power Dissipation.
  • Calculate the PSRR (Power Supply Rejection Ratio).
  • Calculate the Inrush Current
  • Prepare test reports and understand necessary testing steps in LDO circuit.
  • Apply design tips and layout strategies to improve the performance of your LDO circuit.

By the end of this course, you'll have the confidence and knowledge to design a highly efficient and reliable LDO circuit for your projects like an expert.
