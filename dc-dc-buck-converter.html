DC-DC Buck Converter Course

What You'll Learn

This course shares the professional approach to calculating and designing your DC-DC Buck converter. It is based on hands-on knowledge gained from years of experience, not academic training. The course is divided into four main parts: Requirements, Design, Test, and Layout Recommendations.

You will learn:

  • How to read and interpret your specifications correctly.
  • How to choose the right DC-DC converter for your specific application.
  • Component selection, including feedback resistors, inductors, and input/output capacitors.
  • How to check impedance, ESR, and DC bias for proper component selection.
  • Efficiency and thermal considerations.
  • Preparing test reports for your design and understanding key testing requirements.
  • Expert tips and layout recommendations for improved design.
