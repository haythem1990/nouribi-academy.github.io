DC-DC Buck Converter Course

What You'll Learn

This course shares the professional approach to calculating and designing your DC-DC Buck converter. It is based on hands-on knowledge gained from years of experience, not academic training. The course is divided into four main parts: Requirements, Design, Test, and Layout Recommendations.

You will learn:

  • Read and interpret your specifications correctly.
  • Choose the right DC-DC converter for your specific application.
  • Select components such as feedback resistors, inductors, and input/output capacitors.
  • Evaluate impedance, ESR, and DC bias for proper component selection.
  • Address efficiency and thermal considerations to ensure optimal performance.
  • Prepare test reports for your design and understand the key testing requirements.
  • Apply expert tips and layout recommendations to improve your designs.

By the end of this course, you’ll be equipped to design and optimize your DC-DC Buck converter like a professional.
